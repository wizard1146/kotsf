TL;DR — the three things that matter

1 · Critical bug — 8 expedition skill-checks silently always-lost ✓ fixed v37

Every expedition test on power/guile/courage resolved to 0 (the engine's getPath can't read member competence) → P(win)=0. The Champion's Tourney was unwinnable, the endgame flag hearth_accord was unreachable, and the seizure / ambush branches always killed the party.

Fixed: getPath now resolves competence inside an active expedition beat as the best of the party you sent — so "who you send matters." A harness lint now rejects non-resolvable scene paths.

2 · 41 of 47 flags are orphans (set, never read) cheap win

The entire creation backstory (deed_*, belief_*, fear_*, mark_*, path_*, player_*) and every endgame flag (ember_borne, glimpsed_first_memory, hearth_accord) gate nothing. Only 6 flags are actually consumed.

3 · The endgame spine is unbuilt biggest gap

No Ember counter, no Keepers, no Secret Tower, no elevation. Ember-hunt scenes exist but feed no campaign counter — the hunt has no destination.

2b · Distribution across masteries vs pressures

  • Pressures dominate; two schools are never checked. mastery.red and mastery.yellow are never a test path (nor a trigger/requires). Red & Yellow can raise mastery at creation but the coven can never show it off.
  • lore is over-used — 11 of 30 scene tests, 14 tags, the most common trigger path. faith (9) and mana (6) follow. provisions is never a test stat despite two famine scenes.
  • Mastery checks are swingy 0%/35% gates. A coven with no purple hits mastery.purple vs40 at ~0% — effectively "do you own this school", a softer cousin of the 2a bug.

2c · Difficulty spread

Resolvable checks cluster 33–54% at opening pressures — nothing trivially easy, nothing certain (bounded model). Two structural notes: mana-checks trigger when mana is low, so effective odds are worse than base (punishing but on-theme); and several checks reward the same pressure they test (mild rich-get-richer).

4Narrative gaps vs North Star & Endgame Arc

  • The endgame spine is essentially unbuilt. Secret Tower (0 scenes), the three Keepers — mad / power-lost hermit / imprisoned (0 scenes), elevation of 3 Wizards (no subsystem), the Fracture-close 7-choice finale (0 scenes), and the Archon's own tracked stats (absent). None of it exists yet.
  • Ember density vs the 8–14 target. Hunt scenes are stocked, but there's no campaign counter; the flags that should feed it (ember_borne, ember_taken, ember_ally) are orphans. The hunt has no destination.
  • Inter-Circle personality drama is the biggest thin spot. Only 1 primary-coven scene and 1 conditional advisor line in the entire content set. Advisors are static two-voice pairs that rarely react to state, backstory, or each other — under-serving North Star #5 (no perfect info) and #6 (distinct personalities).
  • Warriors / steel-vs-magic is present but shallow; warriors_disdain / warriors_none are orphans, so two of four backstory answers pay off nothing.
  • Recurring characters barely exist — the-old-archons-kin is the one true callback.
