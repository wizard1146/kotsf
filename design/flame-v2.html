What changed

v1 was smooth-vector triangles — wrong idiom (FRWC is pixel-art) and too geometric. This redraw is a hand-placed pixel flame using the actual Sacred Flame colour zones from the NFT: crimson licking tips → orange body → white-hot core → a violet/magenta glow pooling at the base. No rounded strokes, no outline — it glows against the dark like the real one. Pixel art also scales crisply and is genuinely authorable (unlike the grunge-textured hero icons, which want AI-gen — see ICON_PROMPTS.md).
