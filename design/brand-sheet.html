01 The Mark — the Sacred Flame

A pixel-art flame in the idiom of the real Forgotten Runes Sacred Flame. Canon colour zones, top→bottom: crimson tips → orange body → white-hot core → violet/magenta base glow. No outline — it glows against the dark.

Do

Export the favicon at native 32×32; scale at integer multiples only.

Place on warm near-black. Keep one flame-width of clear space.

Use the animated flicker for splash / loading (respect reduced-motion).

Don't

No drop-shadow, bevel, or outline. No smooth-scaling the pixels.

Don't recolour outside the canon zones. Don't rotate or stretch.

Retired: the v1 vector triangles and the old ᚾ box glyph.

04 Iconography — two tiers

Reference bar: Aion Rising badges × Mad Monkies grunge × forgottenrunes.com occult-folk. Bold filled silhouettes, sharp/angular, grunge-textured, framed. Zero rounded "SaaS" strokes. Nothing cutesy.

Tier A — Functional (vector, hand-authored)

The tiny always-on icons: 7 pressures, chrome, feedback. Sharp vector — miter joins, no rounded caps, the flame-tongue motif recurring. Must read at 16–24px. One SVG sprite.

Tier B — Hero / ornate (AI-gen)

Brand flame, the 10 screen runes, cult sigils, achievements. Generated in the Aion/Mad-Monkies idiom, then vectorised. Prompts in ICON_PROMPTS.md; inventory in flame-explorer.html.

One family across both tiers · white/cream on dark · sharp corners · cult icons always keyed to the cult colour.

05 Imagery & art direction

Splash / hero: painterly, cinematic dark-fantasy (see assets/splash.png) — always behind a dark scrim so amber text stays legible.

In-world / scenes: pixel-art, matching the Runiverse (the Flame, holds, the Fracture ash).

Mood: firelit dark, occult, folk-mythic, patchwork-broken world. Never clean high-fantasy gloss, never bright/flat.

Boundary: respect canon/invention — never imply official Forgotten Runes endorsement; keep our invented content clearly ours.

06 Voice & tone

Terse, mythic, a little grim — a saga being remembered, not a UI being narrated. Losses are authored tragedies. Advisors disagree; odds are telegraphed in-fiction.

In voice

"The stores ran dry before the harvest. A coven cannot eat runes."

"And what walks back out wearing our faces? I have buried Souls before, Archon."

Out of voice

"You have 0 Provisions. Game over."

"Success chance: 42%. Confirm action?"

07 Motion & the quick don'ts

Motion

Signature = the flame flicker (3-frame ping-pong). Keep motion restrained — occasional flicker/glow, gentle modal rises. No bouncy/springy easing (reads cutesy). Honour prefers-reduced-motion everywhere.

Never

  • Rounded, thin, "friendly" icon strokes
  • Smooth-scaling the pixel flame (integer scales only)
  • A second / third typeface (Barlow + mono numbers only)
  • A cult colour used as generic UI chrome
  • Light-on-light — the brand lives on warm near-black
  • Implying official Forgotten Runes endorsement
